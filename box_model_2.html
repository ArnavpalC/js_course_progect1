Pizza

Pizza (itaian: [pittsa], Neapolitan: [ptta]) is a savory dish of italian origin consisting of a usually roung, flattened base of leavened wheat based dough topped with tomatos, cheese and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions,olives, pinapple, meat, etc) which is then baked at a high temprature, traditionally in a wood-fire oven [1] A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta . A person who makes pizza is known as a pizzaiolo
